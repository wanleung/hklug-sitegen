Title: Linux Kernel Q2 2026 Recap: Driver Pruning, AI Security Tools, and Other Developments
Content:
<p>Phoronix has published its quarterly recap of Linux kernel developments for Q2 2026. Titled "Linux Dropping Old Drivers, AI Detected Vulnerabilities &amp; Other Kernel Highlights Of Q2," the article highlights several key themes from the past three months in kernel development.</p>
<p>Among the topics covered is the ongoing removal of older hardware drivers from the kernel. This is a familiar pattern in Linux development, where deprecated drivers are periodically cleared out to reduce maintenance burden and keep the codebase focused on currently supported hardware. Such transitions typically follow established deprecation policies, giving users and hardware vendors time to adjust.</p>
<p>Another notable development involves the use of artificial intelligence to detect security vulnerabilities in kernel code. AI-assisted analysis has been gaining ground as a complement to traditional code review and fuzzing techniques, possibly helping developers identify flaws in the kernel's vast and complex source tree more efficiently.</p>
<p>The recap also touches on other general kernel improvements, though specific details on additional optimizations were not accessible at the time of publication. The Linux kernel continues to receive incremental enhancements across various subsystems, contributing to overall stability and efficiency.</p>
<p>These developments underscore the kernel's continuous evolution as it addresses technical debt, explores new security methodologies, and refines core functionality to meet modern infrastructure requirements.</p>
<hr>
<p>Phoronix 發布了其 2026 年第二季 Linux 核心發展的季度回顧。題為「Linux 移除舊驅動程式、AI 偵測漏洞與其他核心重點」的文章，突顯了過去三個月核心開發的數個關鍵主題。</p>
<p>其中一個涵蓋的議題是持續從核心中移除較舊的硬件驅動程式。這是 Linux 開發中的常見模式，即定期清理已淘汰的驅動程式，以減少維護負擔，並讓代碼庫聚焦於目前正在支援的硬件。此類轉變通常遵循既定的淘汰政策，給予用戶和硬件供應商調整的時間。</p>
<p>另一項值得注意的發展涉及使用人工智能偵測核心代碼中的安全漏洞。作為傳統代碼審查和模糊測試技術的補充，AI 輔助分析正日益普及，有機會協助開發者更有效地在核心龐大而複雜的原始碼樹中識別缺陷。</p>
<p>該回顧也提及了其他整體核心改進，但關於額外優化的具體細節在發佈時尚無法獲取。Linux 核心持續在各個子系統接收增量改進，有助於整體穩定性和效率。</p>
<p>這些發展突顯了核心在解決技術債務、探索新的安全方法論，以及精進核心功能以滿足現代基礎設施需求方面的持續演進。</p>
