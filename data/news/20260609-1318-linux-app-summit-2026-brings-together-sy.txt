Title: Linux App Summit 2026 Brings Together systemd Creator Poettering and Universal Blue's Castro in Berlin
Content:
<p>```</p>
<p>The Linux App Summit 2026 took place in Berlin this past May, with slightly more than a dozen talks presented at the event, according to a report by German technology publication Heise.</p>
<p>The programme was symbolically framed between two prominent figures in the Linux world. Lennart Poettering, the creator of systemd, delivered the opening keynote, while Jorge Castro — the initiator of the Universal Blue project, from which the modern Linux systems Bluefin and Bazzite emerge — gave the closing talk.</p>
<p>Heise characterised the gathering as a "Meeting of the Linux Desktop Avant-garde." According to the report, both Castro and Poettering call for a fundamental rethink of how Linux operating systems are delivered.</p>
<p>Beyond those framing details, specifics on the summit's broader content were not immediately available, as the full Heise report requires further access. Further details from this year's gathering may emerge as additional reports are published.</p>
<hr>
<p>根據德國科技刊物 Heise 的報導，Linux App Summit 2026 於今年五月在柏林舉行，活動中進行了十多場演講。</p>
<p>演講節目由 Linux 界兩位具代表性的人物分別打頭陣與壓軸，頗具象徵意義。systemd 的創造者 Lennart Poettering 發表了開幕主題演講；而 Universal Blue 項目（Bluefin 和 Bazzite 等現代 Linux 系統即衍生自該項目）的發起人 Jorge Castro 則進行了閉幕演講。</p>
<p>Heise 將此次聚會形容為「Linux 桌面前衛者的聚首」。報導指出，Castro 和 Poettering 均呼籲對 Linux 作業系統的交付方式進行根本性的重新思考。</p>
<p>除了這些框架性細節外，峰會更廣泛內容的具體資訊暫時無法獲取，因 Heise 的完整報導需要進一步查閱。隨著更多後續報導的發表，本次聚會的進一步細節可能會陸續披露。</p>
