Title: Open-Source Object Storage Seekers Eye Ceph and Garage After MinIO Maintenance Mode Shift
Content:
<p>MinIO's transition to maintenance mode has sparked a search for alternatives in the open-source object storage arena. Announced in late 2025, this move means the widely-used S3-compatible server will no longer accept new features, pushing teams to re-evaluate their storage strategies.</p>
<p>According to a recent analysis on LWN.net, the shift is less a technical failure and more a governance decision, highlighting the importance of long-term viability in open-source dependencies.</p>
<p>For organizations considering their next steps, two primary alternatives have emerged with distinct profiles. Ceph, a veteran in the open-source storage space, offers a unified system capable of providing block, file, and object storage from a single cluster. Its strength lies in managing large-scale, diverse storage needs, though this versatility comes with significantly higher operational complexity.</p>
<p>A more focused contender is Garage, which has gained attention for its simplicity in providing distributed object storage. Designed specifically for this single purpose, it aims to be easier to deploy and manage than Ceph for teams that do not require a multi-protocol storage platform.</p>
<p>For existing MinIO deployments, the immediate impact is minimal; the software continues to function, and critical security patches are expected. However, this announcement serves as a critical trigger for strategic planning, particularly for teams with upcoming infrastructure refreshes.</p>
<p>This scenario underscores a broader lesson: when selecting open-source projects, governance and community health are as vital as technical features. The choice between Ceph's versatility and Garage's simplicity now becomes a pressing consideration for future-proof infrastructure decisions.</p>
<hr>
<p>MinIO 轉入維護模式，引發了開源對象儲存領域對替代方案的搜尋。此舉於2025年底宣佈，意味著這款廣泛使用的、相容 S3 的伺服器將不再接受新功能開發，迫使團隊重新評估其儲存策略。</p>
<p>根據 LWN.net 最近的一篇分析，此轉變與其說是技術失敗，不如說是一個治理決策，突顯了開源依賴方案長期可行性的重要性。</p>
<p>對於考慮下一步行動的組織而言，兩款具有截然不同特點的主要替代方案已經浮現。Ceph 是開源儲存領域的老牌選擇，提供一個能夠從單一叢集提供區塊、檔案及對象儲存的統一系統。其優勢在於管理大規模、多樣化的儲存需求，不過這種多功能性也伴隨著顯著更高的營運維護複雜度。</p>
<p>一個更專注的競爭者是 Garage，因其提供簡潔的分散式對象儲存而受到關注。它專門為此單一目的而設計，對於無需多協議儲存平台的團隊而言，其目標是比 Ceph 更易於部署和管理。</p>
<p>對於現有的 MinIO 部署而言，直接的影響輕微；該軟件繼續正常運作，預期仍會提供關鍵的安全性更新。然而，此公告成為策略規劃的關鍵觸發點，特別是對於即將進行基礎架構更新的團隊。</p>
<p>此情境凸顯了一個更廣泛的教訓：選擇開源項目時，治理與社群健康程度與技術功能同樣至關重要。在 Ceph 的多功能性與 Garage 的簡潔性之間做出選擇，現已成為構建面向未來的基礎架構決策中一項迫切的考量。</p>
