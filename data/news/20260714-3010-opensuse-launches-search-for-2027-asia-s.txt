Title: openSUSE Launches Search for 2027 Asia Summit Host, Highlighting Volunteer Model
Content:
<p>```</p>
<p>The openSUSE Project has officially opened its search for a host community for the 2027 openSUSE.Asia Summit, issuing a call for proposals that emphasizes the event's grassroots, volunteer-led organization. Announced July 14, the initiative invites local free and open-source software (FOSS) groups across the continent to submit detailed bids to organize the annual conference.</p>
<p>The openSUSE.Asia Summit is a key regional event that brings together contributors, users, and FOSS enthusiasts. As outlined in the announcement, it provides a dedicated forum for in-person collaboration, knowledge sharing, and community building within Asia's diverse technical landscape. The 2027 edition aims to continue this tradition, but its future depends entirely on securing a capable and enthusiastic local team.</p>
<p>A core element of the call is the event's reliance on a volunteer-driven model. This approach places full responsibility for planning and execution on the host community, mirroring foundational open-source principles of decentralized coordination. Proposals are due by August 31, 2026, providing an 18-month lead time that the project deems essential for volunteers to manage complex logistics, secure sponsorships, and develop a relevant technical program. This community-centric execution model is common among projects like Debian and Fedora, where flagship events are similarly organized by local volunteer teams rather than a central corporate entity.</p>
<p>For a local community, hosting the summit represents a significant opportunity. It offers a platform to raise its profile, strengthen the regional FOSS network, and directly contribute to advancing the openSUSE project in the strategic Asian market. Interested organizations are expected to outline their capacity, preliminary plans for dates and venue, and a budget framework in their submissions.</p>
<p>The announcement sets broad parameters for proposals but does not enumerate specific evaluation criteria. It also remains to be determined how the openSUSE Board and the global community will formally structure their support for the selected host during the intensive planning phase. These operational details are likely to be addressed as viable proposals are reviewed.</p>
<p>Ultimately, the call for hosts is an invitation for a community to assume stewardship of a major collaborative event. The success of the 2027 summit will hinge on the strength of the grassroots proposal that emerges, showcasing the ongoing commitment to community-led development within the openSUSE project.</p>
<hr>
<p>openSUSE 項目正式展開搜尋2027年 openSUSE.Asia Summit 的主辦社區，並發佈徵集提案公告，強調該活動以草根、志願者主導的組織模式為核心。此項倡議於7月14日宣佈，邀請亞洲各地的自由及開源軟件（FOSS）團體提交詳細競標方案，以籌辦這項年度會議。</p>
<p>openSUSE.Asia Summit 是一項重要的區域活動，旨在匯聚貢獻者、使用者及 FOSS 愛好者。據公告所述，它為亞洲多樣化的技術領域提供了一個專門的實體協作、知識共享及社區建設平台。2027年的版本將延續此傳統，但其未來完全取決於能否找到一個有能力且熱忱的本地團隊。</p>
<p>公告的核心要素在於活動對志願者驅動模式的依賴。此模式將規劃與執行的全部責任賦予主辦社區，反映了開源原則中的分散式協調精神。提案截止日期為2026年8月31日，提供了項目認為對志願者處理複雜後勤、確保贊助及制定相關技術議程至關重要的18個月準備期。這種以社區為中心的執行模式，在 Debian 和 Fedora 等項目中也很常見，其標誌性活動同樣由本地志願者團隊籌辦，而非中央企業實體。</p>
<p>對本地社區而言，主辦峰會代表著一個重大機遇。它提供了一個提升知名度、加強區域 FOSS 網絡，以及直接為 openSUSE 項目在戰略性的亞洲市場作出貢獻的平台。有興趣的組織預計需在提交方案中概述其能力、初步的日期與場地計劃，以及預算框架。</p>
<p>公告為提案設定了廣泛參數，但未詳細列出具體的評估標準。openSUSE 董事會及全球社區如何在密集的規劃階段正式構建對獲選主辦方的支持，仍有待確定。這些操作細節很可能會在可行提案的審議過程中加以解決。</p>
<p>歸根結底，徵求主辦方的公告是對一個社區承擔一項大型協作活動管理職責的邀請。2027年峰會的成功將取決於所湧現的草根提案的強度，從而展示 openSUSE 項目中持續存在的社區主導發展承諾。</p>
