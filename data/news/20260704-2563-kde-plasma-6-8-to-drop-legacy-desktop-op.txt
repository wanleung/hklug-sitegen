Title: KDE Plasma 6.8 to Drop Legacy Desktop OpenGL for Vulkan-Only Rendering
Content:
<p>KDE is set to remove the legacy Desktop OpenGL (GLX) backend from its KWin compositor in the upcoming Plasma 6.8 release, fully transitioning to a Vulkan-based rendering pipeline. This strategic modernization aims to streamline development and align with contemporary Linux graphics standards.</p>
<p>The decision, highlighted in recent developer discussions, marks a major step in simplifying the KWin codebase. By dropping the parallel OpenGL path, KDE developers can consolidate efforts on optimizing a single, modern graphics API. This eliminates a significant maintenance burden and is expected to enhance compositor efficiency.</p>
<p>From a user standpoint, the change will be largely seamless. Vulkan has been a stable, required dependency for several Plasma releases, meaning most supported hardware already meets the requirements. The shift promises potential performance improvements and better support for future display technologies.</p>
<p>This move reflects a broader industry trend away from legacy OpenGL toward Vulkan, which offers more direct and efficient control over GPU resources. By fully embracing Vulkan, KDE reinforces its position as a leader in adopting advanced graphics infrastructure on the Linux desktop.</p>
<p>The development team has reached a consensus on the approach, with no major open questions remaining. The removal of the OpenGL backend will be a key feature of the Plasma 6.8 cycle, further modernizing one of the most prominent open-source desktop environments.</p>
<hr>
<p>KDE 將於即將推出的 Plasma 6.8 版本中，從其 KWin 視窗合成器移除傳統桌面 OpenGL (GLX) 後端，全面轉向基於 Vulkan 的渲染管線。這項策略性現代化舉措旨在簡化開發流程並符合當代 Linux 圖像技術標準。</p>
<p>這一決定在近期開發者討論中被強調，標誌著簡化 KWin 代碼庫的重要一步。通過放棄並行的 OpenGL 路徑，KDE 開發者可以集中精力優化單一現代圖像 API。此舉消除了重大維護負擔，並預期能提升合成器效率。</p>
<p>從用戶角度看，這項變更將基本上實現無縫過渡。Vulkan 在過去數個 Plasma 版本中已是穩定且必需的依賴項，意味著多數受支持硬件已符合要求。這次轉型有望帶來性能提升，並為未來顯示技術提供更佳支持。</p>
<p>此舉反映了業界從傳統 OpenGL 轉向 Vulkan 的更廣泛趨勢——後者能提供更直接、高效的 GPU 資源控制。透過全面擁抱 Vulkan，KDE 進一步鞏固其作為 Linux 桌面領域採用先進圖像基礎架構領先者的地位。</p>
<p>開發團隊已就此方案達成共識，目前無重大懸而未決的問題。移除 OpenGL 後端將成為 Plasma 6.8 開發週期的關鍵特性，進一步推動這個最具影響力的開源桌面環境的現代化進程。</p>
