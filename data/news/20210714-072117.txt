[Hong Kong Open Source Conference - Open to Trust in the New Normal – HKOSCon 2021 is returning on July 17](https://info.hkoscon.org/2021/07/hkoscon2021-press-release/)

Hong Kong Open Source Conference (HKOSCon) is back online! On July 17, 2021 (Sat), 18 selected developers, communities and software builders from Hong Kong, China, Taiwan, Japan, UK and all over the world will share the experience in building trust with peers and moving forward by the deployment of open technologies.
