Title: Ubuntu 26.04 LTS Still Fails to Fix Snapdragon X Elite's Linux Problems
Content:
<p>Fresh benchmarks from Phoronix reveal that the Linux experience on Qualcomm’s Snapdragon X Elite platform has made minimal strides since the release of Ubuntu 26.04 LTS. Testing on an Acer Swift 14 AI laptop confirms that persistent performance gaps and driver immaturity leave the ARM-based system significantly behind contemporary AMD Ryzen AI and Intel Core Ultra competitors running the same distribution.</p>
<p>The evaluation revisits the Snapdragon X Elite hardware after an earlier review late last year had already documented performance regressions and a generally more troublesome experience versus mainstream x86 laptops. The latest results show the situation has not meaningfully improved. Core Linux workloads continue to lag, system drivers remain incomplete, and overall usability introduces friction that most users would rather avoid.</p>
<p>These findings point to deeper structural issues rather than temporary bugs. Gaps in firmware support, underdeveloped kernel-level optimisations, and incomplete driver readiness appear to be limiting factors. While the Snapdragon X Elite silicon is engineered for strong power efficiency under Windows, those advantages have not translated into a polished Linux environment. By contrast, recent AMD and Intel mobile platforms benefit from years of mature upstream kernel development, robust graphics stacks, and sustained vendor engagement that deliver more predictable performance out of the box.</p>
<p>For IT decision-makers and the open-source community, the implications are clear. Organisations prioritising Linux compatibility currently obtain more reliable and higher-performing results by choosing established x86 hardware. The Snapdragon X Elite’s promise of extended battery life and lower power draw is attractive on paper, particularly for teams seeking to reduce energy costs or increase mobile working time. However, until Qualcomm delivers significant improvements in driver and firmware support, those theoretical efficiency gains remain difficult to realise in production Linux use. The hidden costs of troubleshooting, reduced throughput, and compatibility testing can quickly negate any potential operational savings.</p>
<p>The review also highlights the uneven state of ARM-based Linux support outside of a few well-maintained platforms. While community-driven projects have demonstrated that strong vendor cooperation and sustained investment can yield usable results, the Snapdragon X Elite ecosystem has not yet reached that threshold. Without a transparent roadmap from Qualcomm for mainline kernel contributions and long-term driver maintenance, meaningful progress is likely to remain incremental.</p>
<p>Therefore, teams evaluating new laptop fleets have practical reasons to proceed cautiously. Power-efficient ARM designs may eventually deliver measurable cost and sustainability benefits, but present-day Linux readiness still strongly favours AMD and Intel options for most production workloads. Benchmarking actual applications, confirming peripheral compatibility, and assessing long-term kernel support remain essential steps before any large-scale platform shift.</p>
<p>In conclusion, Ubuntu 26.04 LTS has not altered the fundamental picture. The Snapdragon X Elite laptop experience under Linux continues to be lacklustre, reinforcing that mature x86 platforms offer a smoother and more dependable path for users requiring consistent performance and fewer surprises. Until Qualcomm substantially increases its investment in the upstream Linux ecosystem, this ARM platform will struggle to compete on equal footing.</p>
<hr>
<p>Phoronix 發佈的最新基準測試結果顯示，自 Ubuntu 26.04 LTS 發佈以來，Qualcomm Snapdragon X Elite 平台的 Linux 體驗僅實現了微不足道的進步。在一輛 Acer Swift 14 AI 筆記簿電腦上進行的測試證實，持續存在的性能差距和不成熟的驅動程式，導致這個基於 ARM 的系統在運行同一發行版時，明顯落後於同時期的 AMD Ryzen AI 和 Intel Core Ultra 競爭對手。</p>
<p>是次評估在去年底的首次評測之後，再次審視了 Snapdragon X Elite 硬體。之前的評測已記錄了性能回落，以及相較於主流 x86 筆記簿電腦普遍更令人頭痛的體驗。最新的結果表明情況並無實質改善。核心 Linux 工作負載持續落後，系統驅動程式依然不完整，整體可用性帶來的摩擦，是大多數用戶都希望避免的。</p>
<p>這些發現指向更深層的結構性問題，而非暫時性錯誤。固件支援的缺口、未充分發展的核心層級優化、以及未準備就緒的驅動程式，似乎是主要的限制因素。儘管 Snapdragon X Elite 晶片在設計上於 Windows 下具有強勁的電源效率優勢，但這些優勢尚未轉化為一個成熟精緻的 Linux 環境。相比之下，近期的 AMD 和 Intel 行動平台受益於多年來成熟的上游核心開發、穩健的圖形堆棧，以及持續的供應商投入，從而能開箱即交付更可預測的性能。</p>
<p>對於 IT 決策者和開源社區而言，影響是明確的。目前，優先考慮 Linux 相容性的組織，透過選擇成熟的 x86 硬體，能獲得更可靠且性能更高的結果。Snapdragon X Elite 承諾的延長電池續航時間和更低功耗，在紙面上具吸引力，尤其對希望降低能源成本或增加移動工作時間的團隊。然而，除非 Qualcomm 在驅動程式和固件支援方面帶來顯著改進，否則這些理論上的效率增益在生產環境的 Linux 使用中將難以實現。故障排除的隱藏成本、降低的吞吐量和相容性測試，可能迅速抵消任何潛在的營運節省。</p>
<p>評測亦突顯了少數維護良好的平台之外，基於 ARM 的 Linux 支援狀況參差不齊。雖然社區主導的項目已證明，強大的供應商合作和持續投入能產生可用的成果，但 Snapdragon X Elite 生態系統尚未達到該門檻。若缺乏 Qualcomm 對於主線核心貢獻和長期驅動程式維護的透明路線圖，有意義的進展很可能仍將是漸進式的。</p>
<p>因此，評估新筆記簿電腦採購的團隊有務實的理由謹慎行事。節能的 ARM 設計或將最終帶來可衡量的成本和可持續效益，但目前 Linux 的就緒程度，仍強烈有利於 AMD 和 Intel 方案以滿足大多數生產工作負載。在進行任何大規模平台轉移之前，對實際應用程式進行基準測試、確認周邊設備相容性、以及評估長期核心支援，仍然是必要的步驟。</p>
<p>總結而言，Ubuntu 26.04 LTS 並未改變根本局面。Snapdragon X Elite 筆記簿電腦在 Linux 下的體驗仍然乏善可陳，這再次證明成熟的 x86 平台，為需要穩定性能和更少意外的用戶，提供了更順暢且更可靠的途徑。除非 Qualcomm 大幅增加對上游 Linux 生態系統的投入，否則這個 ARM 平台將難以在同一起跑線上競爭。</p>
