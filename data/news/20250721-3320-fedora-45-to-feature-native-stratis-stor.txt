Title: Fedora 45 to Feature Native Stratis Storage Integration in Installer
Content:
<p>The Fedora Engineering and Steering Committee (FESCo) has approved integrating the Stratis storage management system directly into the Anaconda installer for Fedora 45. This decision transforms advanced, ZFS-like features from a post-installation command-line task into a guided, first-class option during system setup.</p>
<p>Stratis, developed by Red Hat, provides a unified storage management layer combining logical volume management and filesystem services. It offers thin provisioning, snapshots, and simplified pool administration atop standard Linux kernel technologies. Previously, using Stratis required manual, expert-level configuration after installation, limiting its accessibility.</p>
<p>The FESCo approval marks a strategic commitment to Stratis, validating its stability and production-readiness within the Fedora ecosystem—a common proving ground for technologies later adopted in Red Hat Enterprise Linux. This move positions Stratis as a peer to established options like LVM and Btrfs, directly within the installer's guided workflow.</p>
<p>By formally offering Stratis as a third integrated volume management choice at installation, Fedora acknowledges the growing trend toward solutions that unify filesystem and volume management. This simplifies administration and reduces the need for stacked tools, reflecting a broader shift in the Linux storage landscape.</p>
<p>While this addresses key adoption barriers, it raises questions about implementation details. How prominently will Stratis be presented in the installer UI? Will it become the suggested default for specific profiles, such as workstations or servers? Users and administrators will also need to evaluate its performance and scalability trade-offs versus Btrfs and traditional LVM for different workloads.</p>
<p>Embedding Stratis into the default Fedora installation experience lowers the barrier for users to experiment with modern, flexible storage practices. It represents a significant evolution of the Anaconda installer, moving beyond basic partitioning to offer sophisticated storage solutions from the first boot, and underscores Stratis's growing role in modern Linux infrastructure.</p>
<hr>
<p>Fedora 工程指導委員會（FESCo）已批准將 Stratis 儲存管理系統直接整合到 Fedora 45 的 Anaconda 安裝程式中。此項決策將原先屬於安裝後需透過命令列執行的高階、類似 ZFS 的功能，轉化為系統設定期間提供的引導式、一等公民選項。</p>
<p>由 Red Hat 開發的 Stratis 提供了統一的儲存管理層，結合了邏輯磁碟區管理與檔案系統服務。它基於標準的 Linux 核心技術，提供精簡配置、快照以及更簡化的儲存池管理功能。以往使用 Stratis 需要在安裝後進行手動且具備專業知識的設定，這限制了其普及性。</p>
<p>FESCo 的批准標誌著對 Stratis 的策略性承認，確認了其在 Fedora 生態系統內的穩定性與生產環境適用性——該系統通常是後續被 Red Hat Enterprise Linux 採用技術的測試平台。此舉將 Stratis 定位為與 LVM 和 Btrfs 等既有選項同等級的方案，直接整合至安裝程式的引導式工作流程中。</p>
<p>Fedora 透過正式將 Stratis 作為安裝時第三個整合式磁碟區管理選項提供，承認了統一檔案系統與磁碟區管理解決方案日益增長的趨勢。這簡化了管理並減少了對堆疊式工具的需求，反映了 Linux 儲存領域更廣泛的轉變。</p>
<p>雖然此舉解決了主要的採用障礙，但也引發了關於實現細節的問題。Stratis 在安裝程式介面中的呈現會有多顯著？它會否成為針對特定配置（如工作站或伺服器）的建議預設選項？使用者和管理員亦需要針對不同工作負載，評估其相對於 Btrfs 及傳統 LVM 在效能與可擴展性方面的取捨。</p>
<p>將 Stratis 內建到 Fedora 的預設安裝體驗中，降低了使用者嘗試現代化、靈活儲存實踐的門檻。這代表了 Anaconda 安裝程式的一次重要演進，超越基礎分割磁碟功能，從首次開機起即提供複雜的儲存解決方案，並突顯了 Stratis 在現代 Linux 基礎設施中日益重要的角色。</p>
