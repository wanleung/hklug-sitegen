Title: AMD-Backed Lemonade Project Hits 11.5 With Multi-Model Router for Local AI Inference
Content:
<p>The Lemonade project, an open-source platform backed by AMD for running AI models locally, has released version 11.5. According to Phoronix reporting, the update was announced alongside the AMD Advancing AI event and delivers the completed Lemonade Router, a major architectural shift that transforms the software from a single-model runner into a true multi-model inference server.</p>
<p>The launch follows quickly on the heels of version 11.0, reportedly released just one week prior. This rapid cadence signals an aggressive push by AMD to mature its open-source AI software stack. The centerpiece of the release, the Router, is now a functional core component. It allows system administrators to manage and serve multiple AI models simultaneously, a critical capability for production environments with diverse and concurrent inference workloads.</p>
<p>This move is a key part of AMD's broader strategy to offer a compelling alternative to cloud-based AI APIs. By enhancing the Lemonade stack, the company provides a pathway for organizations to run AI workloads on-premises on compatible AMD hardware. The emphasis is on practical benefits: stronger data sovereignty, reduced latency, and greater cost predictability.</p>
<p>With the foundational multi-model routing now in place, community attention will pivot to real-world validation. The project's credibility will be defined by independent stress-tests of the Router's performance, stability, and configuration flexibility under mixed-model loads. Success in this phase is essential for Lemonade to be considered a robust choice for complex local AI deployments.</p>
<p>The release marks a critical transition point. The project must now decide whether to continue its rapid feature expansion or shift focus toward performance tuning and stabilization—a necessary step to drive broader enterprise adoption and solidify its place in the local AI infrastructure ecosystem.</p>

<p>由AMD支持的開源平台Lemonade（用於在本地運行AI模型）已發布11.5版本。據Phoronix報道，此更新伴隨AMD Advancing AI活動推出，帶來完成的Lemonade路由器——這是一項重大架構轉變，將軟件從單模型運行器轉變為真正的多模型推理伺服器。</p>
<p>此發布緊隨11.0版本之後，據報於一周前推出。這種快速節奏顯示AMD正積極推動其開源AI軟件棧的成熟。此次發布的核心組件路由器現已成為功能性核心。它允許系統管理員同時管理和服務多個AI模型，這對於具有多樣化和同步推理工作負載的生產環境至關重要。</p>
<p>此舉是AMD廣泛戰略的關鍵部分，旨在為基於雲端的AI API提供引人注目的替代方案。通過增強Lemonade技術棧，該公司為組織提供了一條途徑，使其能夠使用兼容的AMD硬件在本地運行AI工作負載。重點在於實際效益：更強的數據主權、降低的延遲和更高的成本可預測性。</p>
<p>隨著基礎多模型路由的就位，社區關注點將轉向實際驗證。該項目的可信度將取決於獨立壓力測試在混合模型負載下對路由器的性能、穩定性和配置靈活性的評估。此階段的成功對於Lemonade被視為複雜本地AI部署的可靠選擇至關重要。</p>
<p>此發布標誌著一個關鍵轉折點。該項目現在必須決定是繼續快速功能擴展，還是轉向性能調優和穩定化——這是推動更廣泛企業採用並鞏固其在本地AI基礎設施生態系統中地位的必要步驟。</p>
