Title: Linux Foundation Launches Project to Create Open Payment Standard for AI Agents
Content:
<p>The Linux Foundation has launched the x402 Foundation, a new project dedicated to building an open, interoperable payment standard for internet-native artificial intelligence agents and applications.</p>
<p>The initiative targets the growing need for AI systems to autonomously conduct financial transactions online. As agents become capable of booking services, purchasing data, or settling for computational resources, a common protocol is seen as essential to prevent a fragmented landscape of incompatible systems.</p>
<p>Named after the HTTP 402 "Payment Required" status code, the project aims to develop a lightweight, secure protocol enabling any AI agent to transact with any service, independent of the underlying payment system or currency. This could span traditional digital payments and newer forms of digital settlement.</p>
<p>Hosting the project under the Linux Foundation leverages its established model for neutral, vendor-agnostic governance of critical open-source infrastructure. The effort seeks to foster a collaborative space for developers and corporations to prevent a future where proprietary systems from large technology vendors become the de facto standard, creating vendor lock-in.</p>
<p>The technical protocol will be developed through the Foundation's open process. Early participants include cloud providers, payment processors, and AI research organizations, indicating industry recognition of the need for this foundational layer.</p>
<p>The emergence of a standardized protocol for AI commerce has broad implications for technology sectors focused on both fintech and AI development. A common standard could lower barriers for developers building AI-powered services, ensuring they can interoperate within a global ecosystem.</p>
<p>The project represents a foundational effort to build the economic infrastructure for a future where AI agents operate as practical actors in the digital world.</p>
<hr>
<p>Linux基金會近日啟動了x402基金會項目，專注於為網絡原生人工智能代理及應用程序構建一個開放、可互操作的支付標準。</p>
<p>該項目旨在應對AI系統自主進行線上金融交易的日益增長需求。隨著AI代理具備預訂服務、購買數據或結算計算資源的能力，建立通用協議被視為防止出現互不兼容的碎片化系統格局的關鍵。</p>
<p>項目名稱取自HTTP 402「需要付款」狀態碼，旨在開發輕量級、安全的協議，使任何AI代理都能與任何服務進行交易，無需依賴特定的支付系統或貨幣。此標準涵蓋傳統數碼支付及新型數碼結算方式。</p>
<p>在Linux基金會下啟動此項目，利用了其成熟的中立、供應商中立治理模式，以管理關鍵開源基礎設施。此舉旨在為開發者和企業營造協作空間，避免大型科技公司的專有系統成為事實標準，從而形成供應商鎖定。</p>
<p>技術協議將通過基金會的開放流程開發。早期參與者包括雲服務供應商、支付處理商及AI研究機構，顯示業界認可此基礎層的重要性。</p>
<p>AI商貿標準化協議的出現，對金融科技和AI開發領域的技術行業具有深遠影響。統一標準可降低開發者構建AI驅動服務的門檻，確保其能在全球生態系統中實現互操作。</p>
<p>該項目代表了一項基礎性努力，旨在為未來AI代理作為數碼世界的實踐參與者構建經濟基礎設施。</p>
