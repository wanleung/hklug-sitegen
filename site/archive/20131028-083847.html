Open Source Workshop #16 (November 2013)

By HKLUG · 2013-10-28 08:38

Next open source workshop in November will be hosted on November 2, it will be hosted at CItyU Y5-306.

Agenda

  1. Open Source News & Updates by HKLUG and OSHK.
  2. Firefox OS App Manager demo (Gary Kwong, Security Engineer from Mozilla Mountain View office).
  3. Lightning Talks: Tell us your open source project ideas / updates in 5 minutes!
  4. Open discussion and hacking session.

Lightning Talks

You can demostrate or express your open source projects, user experiences, and brainstom ideas to others. And we can form some hacking / discussion group to continue your work/discussion after lightning talks.

Lightning Talks are 5 min each by default.

  • (Please submit your lightning talks to webmaster@opensource.hk to tell us your topics if you got any).

Date: 2013/11/02, Saturday
Time: 2:30-5:30pm
Venue: Classroom Y5-306, 5/F Academic 1, City University of Hong Kong, Kowloon Tong.
Expected no. of attendee: 40-60
RSVP: http://hkosw16.eventbrite.com/

Organizers:

  • Hong Kong Linux User Group.
  • Mozilla Hong Kong Community.
  • Open Source Hong Kong.
