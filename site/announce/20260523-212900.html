Hong Kong Open Source Conference 2026 - 6th June 2026

2026-05-23 21:29:00 | Hong Kong Linux User Group

Hong Kong Open Source Conference 2026 (HKOSCon 2026)

日期 Date： 6th June 2026 (Saturday 星期六)
地點 Venue： Hong Kong Institute of Information Technology, VTC Tsing Yi Complex
香港資訊科技學院，職業訓練局青衣大樓（新界青衣島青衣路20A號）

The annual Hong Kong Open Source Conference is back! Join us for a full day of talks covering AI agents, open source infrastructure, Linux, security, IoT, and more.

香港開源年會又回來了！一整天的講座，涵蓋 AI Agents、開源基礎設施、Linux、資訊安全、物聯網等多個主題。

部分講題 Selected Talks：

  • State of Open Source: Sustainability and Digital Sovereignty — Sammy Fung
  • 由龍蝦仔到Hermes Agent — 阿當
  • BugTraceAI: Open-Source Agentic AI for Autonomous Multi-Agent Bug Bounty & Pentesting
  • 讓龍蝦 Agents 走入現實：AWS x OpenClaw x 機械人 實戰指南 — Cyrus Wong & Phoebe Pang
  • Raspberry Pi in Alcohol Brewing: Modernizing Craft Processes with IoT — Masafumi Ohta
  • Platform Patterns for Agentic AI on Kubernetes — Saurabh Mishra
  • From E-Waste to Education: Porting LineageOS
  • 論壇 - 2026年香港開源社群現況
