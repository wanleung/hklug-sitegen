Title: FreeBSD 15.1-RC1 Includes Fixes for AI-Identified Vulnerabilities
Content:
<p>The FreeBSD Project has released version 15.1-RC1, the first release candidate ahead of a stable launch planned for June. The release includes security fixes for vulnerabilities identified through AI and LLM-driven discovery tools.</p>
<p>The RC addresses flaws found by automated scanning tools and large language models, reflecting a growing trend in AI-assisted security research across open-source ecosystems. FreeBSD maintainers reviewed and validated the AI-flagged issues before incorporating patches into the release candidate.</p>
<p>Users and developers are encouraged to install 15.1-RC1 in non-production environments and report issues through established channels. The testing period helps identify regressions and compatibility problems before the June stable release.</p>
<p>FreeBSD 15.1-RC1 is available for download now.</p>
<hr>
<p>FreeBSD Project 已推出 15.1-RC1 版本，這是六月計劃推出穩定版前的首個候選版本。此版本包含透過 AI 和 LLM 驅動發現工具識別的安全漏洞修補。</p>
<p>該 RC 版本處理了由自動掃描工具和大型語言模型發現的漏洞，反映開源生態系統中 AI 輔助安全研究日益增長的趨勢。FreeBSD 維護人員在將修補納入候選版本前，已審查並驗證 AI 標記的問題。</p>
<p>鼓勵用戶和開發者在非生產環境安裝 15.1-RC1，並透過既定渠道報告問題。測試期有助在六月穩定版推出前識別 regression 和 compatibility 問題。</p>
<p>FreeBSD 15.1-RC1 現已可供下載。</p>
