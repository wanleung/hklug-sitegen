Title: Torvalds Hints Linux 7.1 Final Release Could Ship Next Week After rc7 Drop
Content:
<p>Linux kernel 7.1-rc7 has been released for testing, and if all goes smoothly, it could be the last release candidate before the final stable kernel is published. Linus Torvalds, who oversees kernel development, indicated that barring any unexpected problems, the official 7.1 release could arrive as early as next week.</p>
<p>"Anyway, as things look now this is the last rc," Torvalds wrote in the release announcement. "Something can obviously always come up and force us to change that, but please give rc7 a whirl and keep testing for one more week."</p>
<h4>Understanding the Release Candidate Cycle</h4>
<p>The Linux kernel follows a well-established development cadence. After each major release, a two-week merge window opens during which new features and significant changes are integrated. Once that window closes, a series of release candidates — typically labelled rc1 through rc7 or rc8 — are published at roughly weekly intervals. These candidates are intended for broad testing and bug fixing, with each successive version converging on stability.</p>
<p>Reaching rc7 is a notable milestone. While some kernel cycles extend to rc8 or beyond when serious regressions surface, most conclude at this stage. Torvalds' statement that this is "the last rc" signals his confidence that the current codebase is in good shape.</p>
<h4>Getting Ready for the Final Release</h4>
<p>For distribution maintainers, the arrival of rc7 marks the point to begin validating the new kernel against their packaging and integration workflows. For end users running mainline kernels, rc7 represents the closest approximation to what the final release will look like. Anyone who has been waiting for a stable window to evaluate the 7.1 series now has a near-final candidate to work with.</p>
<h4>Testing Remains Critical</h4>
<p>Despite the optimistic outlook, Torvalds' cautionary note — "something can obviously always come up" — underscores a fundamental reality of kernel development. Even at the seventh release candidate, critical bugs can and occasionally do surface. The entire release candidate phase exists precisely to cast a wide net for regressions, performance issues, and hardware-specific problems that automated testing cannot catch.</p>
<p>Users and developers willing to test rc7 on their systems and report issues through the appropriate mailing lists and bug trackers play a direct role in ensuring the stability of the final release. Compatibility testing across diverse hardware is especially valuable at this stage, as it helps surface problems affecting specific configurations that core developers may not have access to.</p>
<h4>What Happens Next</h4>
<p>If the testing period proceeds without major disruption, the stable Linux 7.1 kernel should land next week. Should a serious regression be discovered, the process would extend with an additional rc8.</p>
<p>For the broader open-source ecosystem — including cloud providers, embedded device manufacturers, and desktop distributions — a new kernel release is a significant event. While most downstream projects will not immediately adopt 7.1, the release sets the foundation for the next generation of Linux-based systems across every segment of the industry.</p>
<hr>
<p>Linux 核心 7.1-rc7 已發佈供測試，若一切順利，這可能是最終穩定版發佈前的最後一個候選版本。負責監督核心開發的林納斯・托瓦茲表示，若無意外狀況，官方 7.1 版本最快可於下週發佈。</p>
<p>「目前看來，這是最後一個 rc 版本，」托瓦茲在發佈公告中寫道。「當然，隨時可能有突發狀況迫使我們改變計劃，但請各位試用 rc7，並繼續測試多一週。」</p>
<h4>理解候選版本週期</h4>
<p>Linux 核心遵循一套成熟的開發節奏。每個主要版本發佈後，會開啟為期兩週的合併窗口，在此期間整合新功能與重大變更。窗口關閉後，通常以大約每週一次的頻率，推出一系列標記為 rc1 至 rc7 或 rc8 的候選版本。這些候選版本旨在進行廣泛測試和修正錯誤，每個後續版本都更趨於穩定。</p>
<p>達到 rc7 是一個重要里程碑。雖然部分核心週期在出現嚴重回歸問題時會延長至 rc8 或更高版本，但大多數會在此階段結束。托瓦茲稱這是「最後一個 rc」的說法，顯示他對當前程式碼庫的狀態充滿信心。</p>
<h4>為最終版本做好準備</h4>
<p>對發行版維護者而言，rc7 的推出標誌著開始根據其打包和整合流程驗證新核心的時刻。對使用主線核心的終端用戶來說，rc7 已是最接近最終版本的形態。任何一直在等待穩定窗口來評估 7.1 系列的人，現在都有一個接近最終版的候選版本可供使用。</p>
<h4>測試仍然至關重要</h4>
<p>儘管前景樂觀，但托瓦茲的警示——「隨時可能有突發狀況」——凸顯了核心開發的基本現實。即使進入第七個候選版本，關鍵性錯誤仍可能、且偶爾確實會出現。整個候選版本階段的存在，正是為了廣泛捕捉自動化測試無法發現的回歸問題、效能問題及特定硬件問題。</p>
<p>願意在自身系統上測試 rc7，並透過相應郵件列表和錯誤追蹤系統回報問題的用戶與開發人員，對確保最終版本的穩定性發揮著直接作用。在此階段，跨多種硬件的相容性測試尤其有價值，因為它有助於發現影響特定配置的問題，而這些配置可能是核心開發人員無法接觸到的。</p>
<h4>後續發展</h4>
<p>若測試期間未出現重大問題，穩定的 Linux 7.1 核心應於下週發佈。若發現嚴重回歸問題，流程將延長並推出額外的 rc8。</p>
<p>對更廣泛的開源生態系統——包括雲端服務供應商、嵌入式設備製造商及桌面發行版——而言，新核心發佈是一件重大事件。雖然大多數下游項目不會立即採用 7.1，但這次發佈為業界各個領域的下一代 Linux 系統奠定了基礎。</p>
