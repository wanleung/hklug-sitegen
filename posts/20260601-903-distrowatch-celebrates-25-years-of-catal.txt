Title: DistroWatch Celebrates 25 Years of Cataloguing the Linux Distribution Ecosystem
Content:
<p>```</p>
<p>DistroWatch, the long-running catalogue of Linux and open-source operating system distributions, has reached its 25th anniversary.</p>
<p>The site marked the occasion in its weekly newsletter edition dated 1 June 2026, as reported by LWN.net. Congratulations were offered to Ladislav Bodnar and "all the others who have kept that resource going," as LWN noted.</p>
<h4>"A Highly Addictive Pastime"</h4>
<p>In his anniversary remarks, Bodnar reflected on the experience with evident enthusiasm. "All in all, it has been an incredible ride," he wrote. He described downloading and testing distributions as "a highly addictive pastime," adding that he has been "an avid distro-hopper for the last 25 years" and does not see himself abandoning the activity any time soon.</p>
<p>The sentiment captures something about the culture DistroWatch both documents and celebrates: a deep commitment to exploration and experimentation that remains a defining characteristic of the open-source community.</p>
<h4>A Quarter Century of Staying Power</h4>
<p>A quarter century is an eternity on the internet, and DistroWatch's persistence speaks to a genuine and ongoing need. The Linux distribution ecosystem remains remarkably diverse, with hundreds of active projects continuing to emerge, evolve, and serve different niches. Keeping track of this landscape without a dedicated index would be impractical for most users.</p>
<p>Bodnar's 25 years of sustained effort represent the kind of personal commitment that keeps independent open-source infrastructure running — a quiet, persistent contribution to the broader free software world.</p>
<p>Congratulations to Ladislav Bodnar and the wider DistroWatch community on a quarter century of service.</p>
<hr>
<p>DistroWatch，這份長期運作、收錄Linux及開源作業系統發行版的目錄，迎來了其25週年。</p>
<p>該網站在其2026年6月1日的每週通訊中標誌了這一時刻，LWN.net對此進行了報導。Ladislav Bodnar及「所有其他讓該資源持續運作的人」獲得了祝賀，LWN如此指出。</p>
<h4>「一項高度令人上癮的消遣」</h4>
<p>在週年感言中，Bodnar以明顯的熱情回顧了這段經歷。「總而言之，這是一段不可思議的旅程，」他寫道。他形容下載和測試各發行版為「一項高度令人上癮的消遣」，並補充說自己已「作為一名狂熱的發行版跳躍者長達25年」，而且不認為自己會在短期內放棄這項活動。</p>
<p>這番話捕捉到了DistroWatch所記錄和頌揚的文化精髓：對探索和實驗的深刻承諾，這始終是開源社群的標誌性特徵。</p>
<h4>四分之一世紀的持久力</h4>
<p>在互聯網上，四分之一世紀堪稱永恆，而DistroWatch的持續存在反映了一種真實且持續的需求。Linux發行版生態系統仍然顯著地多元，數以百計的活躍項目持續湧現、發展並服務於不同的領域。若沒有一個專門的索引，對大多數用戶而言，要跟蹤這片版圖是不切實際的。</p>
<p>Bodnar長達25年的持續努力，代表了那種讓獨立開源基礎設施得以運作的個人承諾——對更廣泛的自由軟件世界的一份默默而堅韌的貢獻。</p>
<p>祝賀Ladislav Bodnar及更廣泛的DistroWatch社群服務四分之一世紀。</p>
