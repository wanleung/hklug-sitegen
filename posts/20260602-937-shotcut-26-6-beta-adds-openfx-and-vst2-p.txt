Title: Shotcut 26.6 Beta Adds OpenFX and VST2 Plugin Support to Open-Source Video Editor
Content:
<p>Shotcut, the widely used open-source video editor, has entered beta testing for version 26.6, introducing support for two industry-standard plugin formats alongside a substantial round of bug fixes. The release, reported by Phoronix on 2 June 2026, marks a notable step forward for the cross-platform tool, which runs on Linux, Windows, and macOS.</p>
<p>The headline feature in this beta is the addition of support for OpenFX and VST2 plugins. OpenFX (OFX) is a widely adopted open standard for visual effects plugins, used across professional compositing and editing applications such as DaVinci Resolve and Nuke. VST2, originally developed by Steinberg for audio processing, remains one of the most common formats for audio effect plugins in production environments. For Shotcut users, the ability to load third-party effects in these formats means the editor can now tap into a large existing ecosystem of freely and commercially available plugins — a capability that has long been standard in commercial editing suites but has been absent from many open-source alternatives.</p>
<p>This addition is significant because it narrows a persistent gap between open-source and proprietary video editing tools. Shotcut has steadily improved its built-in feature set over the years, but the inability to extend functionality through plugins limited its appeal for users who need specialised effects or audio processing chains. By supporting established industry formats rather than inventing a proprietary plugin system, the Shotcut development team is positioning the editor as a more credible option for semi-professional and enthusiast workflows that rely on third-party tools.</p>
<p>Beyond plugin support, the 26.6 beta includes a broad collection of stability and quality-of-life fixes. Among the addressed issues are corrections specific to the Flatpak packaging format — a detail that will be welcomed by the large segment of Shotcut's user base running the application on Linux distributions. Flatpak has become one of the primary distribution channels for desktop Linux applications, and packaging-related fixes help ensure a smoother out-of-the-box experience for users who install Shotcut through this method.</p>
<p>The release also arrives with the usual beta-stage caveats. Users running production workflows are advised to wait for a stable release before upgrading, as beta builds may contain regressions or incomplete implementations of new features.</p>
<p>Shotcut has maintained a steady release cadence since its initial launch, and its development model — centred on community contributions and transparent development through platforms like GitHub — has helped it remain one of the most actively maintained open-source video editors available. The addition of OpenFX and VST2 support in 26.6 suggests the project is moving into a phase where extensibility and interoperability with the broader creative software ecosystem are becoming priorities alongside core editing capabilities.</p>
<p>For IT professionals and creators who work across platforms, this beta is worth monitoring. The ability to use the same plugin libraries on Linux as on other operating systems could streamline workflows in mixed-environment studios and reduce the dependency on commercial editing software for certain tasks.</p>

<p>廣泛使用的開源影片編輯器 Shotcut 已進入 26.6 版本的 Beta 測試階段，除了大量的錯誤修正外，還引入了對兩種業界標準插件格式的支援。據 Phoronix 於 2026 年 6 月 2 日報導，此版本標誌著這個可在 Linux、Windows 和 macOS 上運行的跨平台工具向前邁出了重要一步。</p>
<p>此次 Beta 版的主要亮點是新增了對 OpenFX 和 VST2 插件的支援。OpenFX (OFX) 是一個被廣泛採用的視覺效果插件開放標準，用於 DaVinci Resolve 和 Nuke 等專業合成與編輯應用程式。VST2 最初由 Steinberg 為音訊處理而開發，至今仍是生產環境中最常見的音訊效果插件格式之一。對於 Shotcut 使用者而言，能夠載入這些格式的第三方效果，意味著編輯器現在可以利用一個龐大的、免費或商業化的現有插件生態系統——這項能力在商業編輯套件中早已是標準配置，但在許多開源替代方案中卻一直缺失。</p>
<p>此項新增功能意義重大，因為它縮小了開源與專有影片編輯工具之間長期存在的差距。多年來，Shotcut 穩步改進其內建功能，但無法透過插件擴展功能，限制了它對需要專業效果或音訊處理鏈的使用者的吸引力。透過支援既有的業界標準格式，而非發明專有的插件系統，Shotcut 開發團隊正將這款編輯器定位為一個更可靠的選擇，適用於依賴第三方工具的半專業及愛好者工作流程。</p>
<p>除了插件支援外，26.6 Beta 版還包含了一系列廣泛的穩定性與使用體驗改善修正。其中解決的問題包括針對 Flatpak 打包格式的特定修正——這個細節將受到在 Linux 發行版上運行 Shotcut 的大部分使用者群體的歡迎。Flatpak 已成為桌面 Linux 應用程式的主要分發管道之一，與打包相關的修正有助於確保使用者透過此方法安裝 Shotcut 時獲得更順暢的開箱即用體驗。</p>
<p>此版本也帶有 Beta 階段常見的注意事項。建議在生產工作流程中運行的使用者等待穩定版本發佈後再升級，因為 Beta 版本可能存在回歸錯誤或新功能未完全實現的問題。</p>
<p>自首次發佈以來，Shotcut 一直保持著穩定的更新節奏，其開發模式——以社群貢獻為核心，並透過 GitHub 等平台進行透明開發——幫助它持續成為現有維護最積極的開源影片編輯器之一。26.6 版中加入 OpenFX 和 VST2 支援，表明該專案正在進入一個新階段，擴展性以及與更廣泛創意軟體生態系統的互操作性，正與核心編輯能力一同成為優先事項。</p>
<p>對於跨平台工作的 IT 專業人士和創作者而言，這個 Beta 版值得關注。在 Linux 上使用與其他作業系統相同的插件庫的能力，可以在混合環境的工作室中簡化工作流程，並減少對特定任務依賴商業編輯軟件的程度。</p>
