Title: KDE Plasma 6.8 Set to Drop X11 as Wayland Adoption Hits 95 Percent
Content:
<p>The KDE development community has reaffirmed its commitment to making Plasma 6.8 a Wayland-only release, removing the legacy X11 session entirely — a milestone made all the more plausible by the revelation that roughly 95 percent of current Plasma 6.6 users are already running Wayland as their display server.</p>
<h4>The Numbers Tell the Story</h4>
<p>The adoption figure, reported by Phoronix, underscores a rapid transition that has outpaced many observers' expectations. When KDE first announced plans to retire the X11 session, scepticism was widespread. The 95 percent figure effectively silences the most common objection — that dropping X11 would strand a significant user base.</p>
<p>For the remaining five percent still on X11, the transition path will require attention. Plasma 6.8, when it ships, will simply not offer an X11 session option at login. Users on older or unusual hardware configurations — particularly those reliant on proprietary Nvidia drivers with spotty Wayland support in earlier releases — may need to explore alternative desktop environments or update their driver stacks.</p>
<h4>X11 Apps Are Not Dead</h4>
<p>A critical distinction that often gets lost in discussions of this transition: dropping X11 <em>session</em> support does not mean X11 <em>applications</em> stop working. KDE's continued investment in XWayland — the compatibility layer that lets legacy X11 applications run seamlessly on a Wayland compositor — ensures that older software remains functional.</p>
<p>This matters enormously for enterprise and developer environments where tooling built for the X11 protocol may still be in active use. Terminal emulators, legacy GUI tools, and certain screenshot or screen-sharing utilities will continue to operate under XWayland, though some edge-case functionality (particularly around input handling and global hotkeys) may behave differently than on a native X11 session.</p>
<h4>The Broader Ecosystem Shift</h4>
<p>KDE's move is not happening in isolation. GNOME, the other major Linux desktop environment, already made Wayland its default session years ago and has progressively de-emphasised X11. Distributions such as Fedora and Ubuntu have followed suit, shipping Wayland as the out-of-the-box experience for most users.</p>
<p>What makes KDE's transition particularly noteworthy is the speed of voluntary adoption. The fact that 95 percent of Plasma 6.6 users switched to Wayland without being forced suggests the technology has reached a maturity level where it functions as a drop-in replacement for the vast majority of desktop workloads.</p>
<h4>What This Means for Developers and IT Teams</h4>
<p>For developers and system administrators managing Linux workstations, the practical implications are straightforward:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Audit your toolchain.</strong> Identify any X11-specific utilities or scripts and verify they work correctly under XWayland.</li>
<li><strong>Test hardware compatibility.</strong> Nvidia users in particular should ensure they are running recent driver versions with proper Wayland support.</li>
<li><strong>Update automation and documentation.</strong> Any provisioning scripts or configuration management that references X11 session settings will need revision before Plasma 6.8 arrives.</li>
</ul>
<p>The KDE project's telemetry-driven confidence in this decision reflects a broader industry consensus: the X11 display server, first released in 1987, has reached the end of its practical lifespan as a primary session protocol. Its nearly four-decade run is winding down — not with a dramatic removal, but with a quiet, user-led migration that nearly everyone has already completed.</p>
<hr>
<p>KDE 開發社群再次確認，將計劃於 Plasma 6.8 版本中完全移除傳統的 X11 會話，使其成為僅支援 Wayland 的版本。此里程碑得以實現，很大程度上是因為數據顯示，目前 Plasma 6.6 的用戶中，大約已有 95% 正在使用 Wayland 作為其顯示伺服器。</p>
<h4>數據說明一切</h4>
<p>此採用率數據由 Phoronix 報導，凸顯了一場超出許多觀察家預期的快速轉型。當 KDE 首次宣佈計劃淘汰 X11 會話時，外界普遍持懷疑態度。這 95% 的數字有效地平息了最常見的反對意見——即移除 X11 會使大量用戶陷入困境。</p>
<p>對於仍在使用其餘 5% X11 的用戶，轉型路徑需要關注。Plasma 6.8 正式推出時，將不會在登錄選項中提供 X11 會話。使用較舊或特殊硬件配置的用戶——特別是依賴專有 Nvidia 驅動程式的用戶，其在早期版本中的 Wayland 支援並不穩定——可能需要考慮使用其他桌面環境或更新其驅動程式套件。</p>
<h4>X11 應用程式並未消亡</h4>
<p>在此類轉型討論中，一個關鍵的區別常被忽略：移除 X11 <em>會話</em>支援，並不意味著 X11 <em>應用程式</em>無法運作。KDE 對 XWayland 的持續投入——即允許傳統 X11 應用程式在 Wayland 合成器上無縫運行的兼容層——確保了舊軟件仍能正常運作。</p>
<p>這對於企業和開發者環境至關重要，因為這些環境中可能仍在積極使用基於 X11 協議構建的工具。終端模擬器、傳統 GUI 工具以及某些截圖或螢幕共享工具將繼續在 XWayland 下運行，儘管某些邊緣情況的功能（特別是輸入處理和全域快速鍵方面）可能與原生 X11 會話下的行為有所不同。</p>
<h4>更廣泛的生態系統轉變</h4>
<p>KDE 的舉動並非孤立事件。另一個主要的 Linux 桌面環境 GNOME，多年前已將 Wayland 設為預設會話，並逐步減弱對 X11 的重視。諸如 Fedora 和 Ubuntu 等 distribution 亦跟隨此趨勢，為大多數用戶提供開箱即用的 Wayland 體驗。</p>
<p>KDE 的轉型尤為值得注意之處在於自願採用的速度。95% 的 Plasma 6.6 用戶在非強制情況下轉用 Wayland，這表明該技術已達到成熟水平，能夠作為絕大多數桌面工作負載的直接替代方案。</p>
<h4>對開發者及 IT 團隊的意義</h4>
<p>對於管理 Linux 工作站的開發者和系統管理員而言，實際影響非常明確：</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>審查你的工具鏈。</strong> 識別所有 X11 特定的實用工具或腳本，並驗證它們在 XWayland 下是否能正確運作。</li>
<li><strong>測試硬件兼容性。</strong> 特別是 Nvidia 用戶，應確保其運行的是具備適當 Wayland 支援的較新驅動程式版本。</li>
<li><strong>更新自動化腳本和文件。</strong> 任何引用 X11 會話設置的配置腳本或管理文檔，都需要在 Plasma 6.8 推出前進行修訂。</li>
</ul>
<p>KDE 項目基於遙測數據對此決定的信心，反映了一個更廣泛的行業共識：始於 1987 年的 X11 顯示伺服器，作為主要會話協議的實際壽命已走到盡頭。其近四十年的運行即將落幕——並非以戲劇性的移除告終，而是透過一場安靜、由用戶主導的遷移完成，而幾乎所有人都已完成此遷移。</p>
